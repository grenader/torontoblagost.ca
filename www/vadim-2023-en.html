A Dialogue of Vadim Tuneev and Rami Bleckt in Toronto

Russian

A Wise Dialogue

“From Anxiety to Harmony: Inner equilibrium in a modern world”

September 16th 2023 @ 1pm, Toronto

Have you noticed how often worries and stresses come? Why is happiness so fickle? And, how is it possible to attain serenity in an ever changing world? We invite you to take part in a public conversation between two experienced philosophers and psychologists to understand both these questions and to get guidance so you can finally come to your inner peace. Are you ready to join in?

About the event

After the Meet you will:

  • Know where stress and life difficulties come from
  • Discover how a modern world forms them and ways to attain inner peace
  • Understand how to know true happiness through spiritual growth and meaning of life
  • Discuss an art of harmonious relationships and how to enjoy each moment of your life.

During this meet:

  • You will receive true means to overcome stress and worries
  • Understand why and how to grow your spiritual life in this world
  • Master ways to find joy in your every day and successfully get through modern challenges.
